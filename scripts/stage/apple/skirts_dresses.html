Dresses

More Details

If you're a thin Apple body shape you'll be able to dodge past most of the restrictions but your main closet strategy is to trim that waist. Wear clothes that will in a way minimize and make your waist appear thinner. Dresses that skim down over your midsection without clinging.

  • Empire waist
  • Bias cut hem
  • Wrap styles tailor your midriff by visually lifting up your bust
  • Low'ish necklines that slim your upper body and draw the eye up and down
  • Shoulder, neckline and sleeve details
  • Structured shoulders to keep the eye on and above your bust
  • A hemline that flares outwards to balance out your top heavy body, such as an A-line
  • Diagonal lines and big prints
  • Fit-and-flare dresses are lovely when the waist is not too tight, hit the midsection in the right spot, and the fabric does not cling
